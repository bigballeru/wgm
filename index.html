Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our digital display program

How much does it cost to host a display?

There is absolutely no cost to host one of our digital displays. We provide the equipment, installation, and maintenance completely free of charge. In exchange, your business receives free advertising across our network.

What type of displays do you install?

We install high-quality commercial-grade digital displays ranging from 40" to 75" depending on your location. All displays are professionally mounted and include protective features for commercial environments.

How much advertising do I receive as a host?

Host businesses receive $5,000 worth of advertising credit annually, which is distributed across entire network of displays. Your ads will appear in rotation at 5 other locations, giving you extensive exposure throughout the area.

What are the requirements for hosting a display?

Ideal host locations have good foot traffic, are open regular business hours, and have suitable wall space for mounting a display. You'll need to provide a standard power outlet and an internet connection. We handle everything else.

How long is the hosting commitment?

We ask for a minimum 6-month commitment for hosting a display. After that period, the agreement continues month-to-month and can be terminated with 30 days' notice.

Can I control what content appears on my display?

While we manage the advertising rotation, we ensure all content is family-friendly and appropriate for all audiences. We do not display controversial or adult-oriented content. Host businesses can request to block specific ads if needed. We will not advertise any business that is in direct competition with the host business.
